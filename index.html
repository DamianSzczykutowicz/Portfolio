02 About

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at the Rutgers Honors College, drawn to the point where hardware meets software — designing structures, then bringing them to life with embedded systems and control.

My work spans CAD modeling and FEA simulation (SolidWorks, ANSYS), data-driven analysis in MATLAB and Python, and embedded integration with platforms like Arduino, STM32, and the Jetson Nano. I like problems that demand both a clean mechanical design and a system that actually works in the field.

When I'm not in the shop or the lab, I'm usually tinkering with a side project or refining the next iteration of a part.
